The Ides of DEFCON

A wearable hardware badge, featuring blinky lights, sound, a sub-1Ghz radio, games, and more. Based on the Freescale KW01.

Hackaday page

Our Hackaday.io page has our project log, some build instructions, and our current status report.

Kickstarter

Throw us a tip or buy a badge on our Kickstarter page!

Source code and PCB

If you want to make your own board, you can get our PCB layout and Firmware source code.

You will need the GNU ARM Toolchain to build this code, as well as a suitable ARM JTAG/SWD programmer such as the Olimex ARM-USB-OCD-H (see below)
